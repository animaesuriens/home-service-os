**THE BRIDGE:**
Here's how Zapier handles this behind the scenes for your home service company.

When a new contact hits your system, Zapier first grabs all their information and prepares it for processing. Then it runs two simultaneous searches in HubSpot — one looking for their contact ID if they're returning, and another searching by email address to catch any existing records.

The smart part happens next. Zapier evaluates both search results and decides which existing contact to use, or determines this is genuinely someone new. If it finds a match, it carefully merges the incoming data with what you already have, keeping the most recent information while preserving your sales notes and history.

Finally, Zapier either updates the existing contact with the fresh details or creates a brand new record if this person is truly new to your database. The whole process happens in seconds, and your sales team never sees the messy work happening behind the scenes.
