Here's how Zapier makes this happen behind the scenes. When a new lead comes in, the system first takes a quick pause to let everything settle, then loops through all their engagement data to build a complete picture. It generates a payload with all the lead information, then does the detective work your team used to do manually.

The automation runs two parallel searches in HubSpot — one by contact ID and another by email address. This catches contacts who might have changed emails or been imported with different identifiers. Zapier then selects whichever search found a match and merges that existing contact data with the new information.

But here's where it gets smart. The system doesn't just blindly overwrite everything. It loops through any existing deals associated with that contact, checking project types and marketing sources to make sure nothing gets lost in the merge. If the contact came from a specific marketing channel, that gets preserved. If they're tied to an active project, those details stay intact.

The automation branches based on what it finds. New contact? It creates a fresh record with all the proper fields populated. Existing contact? It updates their information while keeping their history. Multiple contacts that might be the same person? It intelligently merges them based on the strongest data match.

Finally, Zapier double-checks that all the required properties exist in the final contact record. No more incomplete profiles or missing phone numbers that leave your sales team scrambling.
