Here's how Zapier makes this seamless:

The second your customer signs digitally, Zapier receives that contract event instantly. No email checking required — the system knows immediately.

Zapier then pulls all the contract records from your database, grabbing every detail about the signed job. It loops through any addendums or change orders, formatting dates and pulling each addendum record so nothing gets missed.

Next comes the smart routing. Zapier branches based on your contract details — is this residential repaint, commercial new construction, or specialty work? Each path triggers different workflows.

For residential jobs, it kicks off crew scheduling, material orders, and your homeowner communication sequence. Commercial work gets the full project management treatment — permit tracking and contractor coordination.

All these setup processes run in parallel, then merge back together when complete. Your job officially moves from "signed contract" to "active project" status.
