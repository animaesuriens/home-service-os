The signed contract triggers the Zap instantly. Zapier pulls all the contract records and job details from your database, then loops through any addendums or change orders, formatting dates and grabbing each record.

Next comes the smart routing. Zapier branches based on the contract details — is this residential repaint, commercial new construction, or specialty work? Each path triggers its own setup process.

For residential jobs, Zapier kicks off crew scheduling, material orders, and the homeowner communication sequence. Commercial projects get the full project management workflow, permit tracking, and contractor coordination.

All these steps flow through Zapier's automation pipeline, processing each piece in sequence, then converging when complete. The job officially moves from signed contract to active project status.
