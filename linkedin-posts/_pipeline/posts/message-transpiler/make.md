Your team is manually looking up customer deals, copying booking links, and shortening URLs before every message. During peak season, this takes forever and customers wait.

Here's what happens instead: Every outbound message automatically pulls deal context, generates the perfect booking link, and delivers it in seconds. No manual lookups, no delays.

Here's how it works behind the scenes in Make:

When staff hits send on a customer message, Make catches that trigger and immediately starts pulling context. The system searches HubSpot for the customer's latest open deal, grabbing current stage, value, and project details.

Next, Make generates a personalized appointment scheduler link based on that deal context. Different deal stages get different booking flows - new prospects get one experience, existing customers get another.

The system then shortens that long booking URL into a clean, trackable link. Makes your messages look professional and lets you track clicks.

Now comes the smart part: Make checks the deal stage and customer type to determine the right message template. New prospects get introduction copy with basic booking instructions. Existing customers get familiar tone with deal-specific details and streamlined flow.

Make's modular canvas makes this branching logic crystal clear - you can see exactly how each customer type flows through the system.

The final message includes deal context, personalized booking link, and proper formatting. What used to take your team 3-4 minutes per message now happens automatically in seconds.

Your customers get the right link faster, your team focuses on higher-value work, and nothing falls through the cracks during busy periods.
