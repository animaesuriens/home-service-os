Your estimates are trapped in one system while your deals live in another. Sales managers spend hours copying numbers between tools, and inconsistent naming makes pipeline reports worthless.

What if every estimate automatically became a perfectly named HubSpot deal the moment you created it? No manual copying, no pipeline gaps, no spreadsheet gymnastics.

Here's how n8n connects your estimating tool to HubSpot:

When you create a new estimate, n8n immediately pulls all quotes from the last few minutes. This catches everything, even if multiple estimates come in at once.

The system loops through each quote individually. Johnson Kitchen Remodel, $12,500. Martinez Bathroom, $8,200. Every estimate gets processed.

Before creating anything, n8n checks if this exact estimate already exists as a HubSpot deal. This prevents duplicates when the automation runs multiple times.

For new estimates, n8n creates a HubSpot deal with consistent naming: customer name, project type, estimate value. Your sales team sees it instantly in their pipeline.

The result? Every estimate becomes a trackable deal automatically. Sales managers get accurate pipeline reports without touching a spreadsheet.

Your estimating tool talks. HubSpot listens. The pipeline updates itself.

n8n's flexible node-based approach gives you complete control over this workflow. You can customize the logic and add complex conditions without being locked into rigid templates.
