Here's how Zapier connects your estimating tool to HubSpot:

When you create a new estimate, Zapier immediately pulls all quotes from the last few minutes. This catches everything, even if multiple estimates come in at once.

The system loops through each quote individually. Johnson Kitchen Remodel, $12,500. Martinez Bathroom, $8,200. Every estimate gets processed.

Before creating anything, Zapier checks if this exact estimate already exists as a HubSpot deal. This prevents duplicates when the automation runs multiple times.

For new estimates, Zapier creates a HubSpot deal with consistent naming: customer name, project type, estimate value. Your sales team sees it instantly in their pipeline.

The result? Every estimate becomes a trackable deal automatically. Sales managers get accurate pipeline reports without touching a spreadsheet.

Your estimating tool talks. HubSpot listens. The pipeline updates itself.
