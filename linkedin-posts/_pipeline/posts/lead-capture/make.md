Your sales rep is copying form submissions into HubSpot at 11pm on a Sunday. Again. Meanwhile, that hot lead from Friday afternoon is still sitting in your inbox, getting colder by the hour.

Here's what happens instead: Every lead flows automatically from any marketing channel straight into your CRM as a complete contact record with deal and source data attached. Even at 2am.

Here's how it works behind the scenes in Make. The moment a form submission hits your system, Make's modular canvas springs into action across 24 connected steps.

First, the lead data arrives from any marketing channel. Could be your website form, a vendor portal, or a campaign landing page. Make doesn't care what time it is.

Next, the system pulls project details from the original source. Residential remodel or commercial build — the context gets captured. Then it traces the marketing attribution. Which campaign drove this lead? Which vendor? The source data gets preserved.

Now comes the smart part. Make searches HubSpot by email and contact ID to check if this person already exists. Existing customer or brand new prospect — the system knows the difference.

If it's an existing contact, Make merges the new project details with their existing record. No duplicate contacts, no lost history. If it's a fresh prospect, Make builds a complete contact record with full project context from day one.

Finally, Make attaches a deal record containing project type, estimated value, and original lead source data. Your HubSpot gets updated with a clean contact record and attached deal within minutes of form submission.

The whole flow runs automatically through lookup steps, enrichment logic, and deduplication branches. Your CRM stays clean and your sales team gets complete lead context instantly.
