The automation starts by receiving events from your CRM whenever deals change status, new leads come in, or existing prospects take action. It immediately grabs the configuration variables that tell it which metrics matter most to your business.

Then it loops through each data item, processing updates in batches to keep everything running smoothly. The system adjusts timestamps to ensure data accuracy, converting everything to epoch time for precise tracking. It generates the exact payload needed to sync deal information with your dashboard.

Here's where it gets smart — the automation processes deletions separately from updates. When deals get removed or archived in your CRM, they disappear from your dashboard instantly. No ghost data cluttering your reports.

The upsert function handles all the heavy lifting, updating existing records and creating new ones as needed. Make branches the workflow based on specific conditions — if it's a new lead, it follows one path. If it's an updated deal, it takes another route. This keeps your dashboard accurate without duplicating data.
