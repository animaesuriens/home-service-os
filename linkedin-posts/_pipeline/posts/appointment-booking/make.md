When someone books through your appointment scheduler, Make immediately grabs the booking event with their email, project type, and time slot. 

First, it checks who owns this customer relationship in HubSpot and pulls their project preferences. Then it builds a complete deal payload combining the booking details with existing contact data.

Here's where it gets smart: Make checks if there's already a HubSpot deal for this customer and project type. If it's a new customer, it creates a fresh deal, sets the stage to "Appointment Scheduled," and links everything together. If they're rescheduling, it updates the existing deal and moves it to the right pipeline stage without creating duplicates.

Finally, Make syncs the contact record with the latest booking information and project details. Your HubSpot dashboard shows the current appointment status, and your sales team sees exactly where each customer stands in the pipeline.
