Here's what happens instead: customers book their own time slots through your appointment scheduler, and every booking automatically updates contact records, deal stages, and project tracking across your entire system. Your team sees exactly where each customer stands without touching a single data field.

When someone schedules through your appointment scheduler, n8n captures that booking event with the customer's email, project type, and time slot. The system immediately looks up who owns this customer relationship and pulls their project preferences from your records.

Next, n8n builds a complete deal payload by combining the booking details with existing contact data and project specifications. Before creating anything new, it checks whether there's already a HubSpot deal for this customer and project type.

If it's a brand new customer, n8n creates a fresh deal, sets the stage to 'Appointment Scheduled,' and links everything to the contact record with full project details. If they're rescheduling or booking additional work, it updates the existing deal instead — moving it to the right pipeline stage and updating the appointment time without creating duplicates.

Throughout this entire process, n8n syncs the contact record in HubSpot with the latest booking information and project preferences. By the time you check your dashboard, the deal stage shows the current appointment status and your sales team sees exactly where each customer stands in the pipeline.
